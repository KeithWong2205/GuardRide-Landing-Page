Who we are

GuardRide is made by enthusiasts, for enthusiasts. We aim to make riding a more enjoyable and safer activity everywhere, everytime for everyone.

Seamless - Worldwide connection

Our devices connection are autonomous and services is available to every corner of the globe. This will ensure ease of mind usage and performance of our product anywhere.

Easy install - Space saving

GuardRide devices are design to be compacted and easy to install with proprietary wiring for specific vehicles ranging from motorcycles, to cars, ATVs and trucks.
